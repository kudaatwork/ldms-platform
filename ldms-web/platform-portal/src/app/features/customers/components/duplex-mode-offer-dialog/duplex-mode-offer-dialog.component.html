Duplex mode lets one legal entity operate as both supplier and customer under a single organisation account. Your trading relationship with them stays intact: where they supply you they remain your supplier; where you register them here they become your customer.

  • Primary classification stays Supplier — no duplicate organisation is created.
  • They can be linked to many suppliers and customers across the platform.
  • They may switch between supplier and customer workspaces after sign-in.
