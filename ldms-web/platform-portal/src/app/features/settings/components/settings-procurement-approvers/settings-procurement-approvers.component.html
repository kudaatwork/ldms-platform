How procurement approvals work

When a requisition is submitted, it moves through the configured number of approval stages in sequence. Each stage must be completed by a different approver before the requisition advances. Once all stages are approved the requisition is marked Approved and a purchase order is generated.

Platform billing for procurement

Each approval stage on a requisition, purchase order, or sales order is tracked as a platform action. On a prepaid wallet, a small charge is deducted from your balance every time an approver completes a stage (see Settings → Platform billing → Usage & deductions). Subscription organisations still see these events in usage reports without per-action deductions.
